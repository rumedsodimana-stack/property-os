Key Features

Everything in one credential

📡
NFC Room Access
ISO/IEC 14443 compatible. Tap to unlock your room, gym, spa, and pool — no physical keys or PINs needed.
🍎
Apple Wallet
PassKit integration delivers your room pass directly to iPhone Wallet. Express mode works even with a drained battery.
◆
Loyalty Card Combined
Your loyalty tier, points balance, and redemption history all embedded. Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond tiers.
🔗
TravelBook Account Link
Card is cryptographically linked to your TravelBook account. Lost card? Revoke and reissue instantly from the app.
🔒
Encrypted & Secure
AES-128 encryption on the NFC chip. Each tap generates a one-time authentication token — clone-proof by design.
🪟
Glass Material
Premium borosilicate glass body with embedded NFC antenna. Durable, eco-friendly, and distinctive — a card guests keep.
