# April Revenue Playbook

Date: 2026-03-31

Goal: Generate the fastest realistic path to cash using the assets already in this repo plus external marketplaces.

## Revenue Lanes

### 1. Hotel AI Reservations + Concierge Pilots

- What we sell: paid pilot for independent hotels and small management groups
- Deliverables: AI concierge, guest request routing, upsell flows, analytics, branded assistant
- Best fit in repo: `services/intelligence/agentService.ts`, `services/operations/guestActionService.ts`, `services/payments/paymentService.ts`, `services/integrations/otaService.ts`
- Price target: USD 10,000-25,000 upfront per pilot
- This-month target: USD 20,000-50,000 if 2-3 pilots close
- Why it wins: strongest product fit and highest ticket

### 2. Freelance AI Automation / Voice Agent Builds

- What we sell: custom AI receptionist, chat agent, CRM automation, scheduling automation
- Channels: Upwork, Freelancer, Contra, direct outbound
- Price target: USD 2,500-10,000 per project
- This-month target: USD 10,000-30,000 if 2-4 projects close
- Why it works: fastest way to use current skills without a long sales cycle

### 3. Open-Source / Startup Bounties

- What we sell: code shipped against a scoped issue or feature bounty
- Channels: Algora, IssueHunt, Gitcoin, direct maintainer outreach
- Price target: USD 300-5,000 per bounty
- This-month target: USD 2,000-12,000 if volume is high
- Why it works: cash can be faster than enterprise sales, but ticket size is smaller

### 4. Security / Bug Bounty Work

- What we sell: vulnerability findings with valid reports
- Channels: HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Google VRP, direct programs
- Price target: highly variable, often zero until a valid finding lands
- This-month target: USD 0-20,000+
- Why it works: upside exists, but near-term predictability is weak

### 5. White-Label / Reseller AI Partner Business

- What we sell: implementation and resale for hospitality AI tooling, plus branded services
- Channels: partner programs and certified partner networks
- Price target: USD 5,000-20,000 per setup or reseller-led deployment
- This-month target: USD 5,000-25,000 if one partner route converts
- Why it works: leverages someone else's trust and customer base
