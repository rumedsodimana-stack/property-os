Signing In — Step by Step

1

Navigate to the Operations App

Open /app in your browser. You'll see the Singularity OS login screen with a dark cosmic background and the ⌘ icon.

2

Enter your Employee ID

This is assigned during enrollment (e.g. GM001, FD001) or by your General Manager. It is not your email address.

3

Enter your 4–6 digit PIN

Your daily login PIN is separate from your email password. It was set during enrollment or by your GM. PINs are 4–6 numeric digits.

4

Click "Access System"

The system verifies your identity and routes you to your role-specific dashboard automatically.

🔐

Demo mode: If VITE_ENABLE_DEMO_USERS=true is set, demo credentials appear on the login screen. Use GM001, FD001, FIN001, or GUEST001 with PIN 1234.

Night vs. Light Mode

🌙

Night Themes

Midnight, Ocean, Sandstone, Aurora, Graphite, Operator, Liquid Glass — all use a deep #09090b dark background. Perfect for low-light operations environments and evening use. All UI elements adapt to use the selected accent colour.

☀️

Codex Light Mode

The Codex Light (operator_light) theme flips the entire Operations App to a clean white #f8f8fc background. All hardcoded dark Tailwind classes are remapped automatically. Ideal for bright lobby environments.

🎨

Theme preference is saved automatically to localStorage. Your last chosen theme persists across page reloads and browser sessions.

Onboarding a new hotel

Property Enrollment

New hotel properties are enrolled through the 6-step Enrollment Wizard accessible from the marketing website's "Enroll" button.

1️⃣

Plan Selection

Choose from Starter, Professional, or Enterprise. All plans include all 16 modules — only room count limits differ. A 50% launch discount applies for the first 3 months.

2️⃣

Hotel Details

Enter your hotel name, room count, country, timezone, and currency. The wizard validates room count against your chosen plan's limits.

3️⃣

Domain Setup

Choose a free Singularity subdomain (yourhotel.singularityos.com) or bring your own domain. Custom domain availability is checked in real time.

4️⃣

Admin Account

Create the GM's email login and a 4–6 digit daily PIN. Password must meet complexity requirements (uppercase + numbers required). The GM's Employee ID will be GM001.

5️⃣

Review & Confirm

Review all details — plan, hotel info, domain, and admin credentials — before submitting. All fields are validated once more at this step.

6️⃣

Provisioning

Singularity creates your isolated cloud tenant, seeds your database, and sends a welcome email. The whole process takes under 15 seconds.

⚠️

Write down your Employee ID (GM001) and PIN immediately after enrollment. There is no self-service PIN reset — contact Singularity support if locked out.

VITE_FIREBASE_* Firebase project configuration —
🚀

To run the app locally: copy .env.template to .env.local, fill in your Firebase and Gemini keys, then run npm install && npm run dev. The app starts on port 3005 by default.

Powered by Gemini

AI Concierge — Ali

Ali is the AI Concierge embedded in the Guest App. Powered by Google Gemini 1.5 Flash, Ali operates as the guest experience architect of Hotel Singularity.

🧠

Intent Detection

Ali automatically detects the type of guest request from natural language — Housekeeping, Maintenance, Front Desk, F&B, or General — and routes it to the correct department.

😊

Sentiment Analysis

Every message is scored on a 0–10 valence scale (0 = frustrated, 10 = delighted). This feeds into the Reputation Dashboard for real-time guest satisfaction monitoring.

🍽️

Halal-First F&B

Given Hotel Singularity's Manama, Bahrain context, Ali always suggests Halal dining options by default when guests ask about food.

⚡

Instant Dispatch

Once intent is confirmed, Ali creates the appropriate task or work order in real time and notifies the guest that it has been dispatched.

🔑

Ali requires a valid VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY to function. Without it, the concierge shows an offline message and gracefully falls back to manual service requests.
